# Exploring the Psychological Puzzle of Travelling: A Journey Through Your Thoughts

**Author:** Maria Aziz
**Affiliation:** Department of Public Administration, University of Karachi
**ORCID:** [0009-0009-2321-4750](https://orcid.org/0009-0009-2321-4750)

## Overview

This research explores the psychological dimensions of daily commuting — how routine
travel (walking, biking, driving, or public transport) shapes our thoughts, emotions, and
mental well-being. The study examines how commuters experience moments of reflection
and peace alongside stress and frustration caused by traffic jams, delays, and crowded
transport, particularly within the context of a megacity like Karachi.

**Keywords:** Psychological Puzzle, Travelling, Thoughts, Traffic
